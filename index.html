About Us

We are the
Scarlet Spacehawks.
Our team represents Illinois Institute of Technology in the NASA Robot Mining Competition at the Kennedy Space center.
Apart from participating in NASA's competition, our mission is to actively reach out to the youth, and inspire the minds of our future STEM leaders and innovators.

"Lofty"

Lofty competed at the Kennedy Space center on May 2017 against 47 other universitites. He underwent many upgrades during the competition, which all played a role in his performance.

Results
